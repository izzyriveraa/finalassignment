ABOUT US

The Rivera Family consists of Dennis, King, Izzy, and Joy. The four of them thoroughly enjoy getting boba together. It is an activity that they often parttake in before dinner time! Some of their favorite boba places are Bambu and Purple Kow. When they got their dog, Chewy, they decided to merge the two things they love most!

Chewy is a full-bred Shih Tzu that entered the Riveras lives around March 2020! Chewy was only a few weeks old when Dennis and Joy drove across thecountry to pick up Chewy from Missouri. Ever since then, he has been with the Rivera family and enjoys every second of it! Chewy appreciates being the face of Chewy Boba.
